Committees

Explore the diverse committees of ARELMUN'25 and their agenda items

Our Committees

ARELMUN'25 features a wide range of committees simulating various UN bodies and international organizations. Click on any committee to view its agenda items and details.

General Assembly

GA1 - DISEC

DISARMAMENT AND INTERNATIONAL SECURITY COMMITTEE

Agenda Item 1

Addressing territorial disputes and ensuring the security of maritime routes in the South China Sea, with a focus on preventing military escalation

Agenda Item 2

Strengthening international efforts to achieve the denuclearization of North Korea and ensuring compliance with non-proliferation treaties

Agenda Item 3

Preventing the Militarization of Space: Developing a Global Treaty to Ban the Use of Space for Military Purposes

GA2 - ECOFIN

ECONOMIC AND FINANCIAL AFFAIRS COUNCIL

Agenda Item 1

Evaluating how the EU's new carbon tariff policies affect global trade, particularly for emerging economies heavily reliant on high-carbon exports

Agenda Item 2

Regulating Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain Technology: Addressing the risks and opportunities of decentralized finance in the global economy

Agenda Item 3

Suez Canal Disruptions and Global Trade: Examining the economic impact of shipping blockages and proposing solutions to prevent future trade disruptions

GA3 - SOCHUM

SOCIAL AND HUMANITARIAN COMMITTEE

Agenda Item 1

Protecting and Preserving the Cultural Heritage of Regions Affected by Conflict: Focusing on the Middle East

Agenda Item 2

The ongoing human rights abuses against ethnic minorities, including the Rohingya, and the impact on the global refugee crisis in Myanmar

Agenda Item 3

Combatting Gender-Based Violence in Conflict Zones: Ensuring Justice for Survivors

GA6 - LEGAL

LEGAL COMMITTEE

Agenda Item 1

Establishing a Global Legal Framework for Regulating Autonomous Systems and Artificial Intelligence in Cross-Border Jurisdictions

Agenda Item 2

Addressing the lack of legal consideration on gene engineering and protection of genetic data

Agenda Item 3

Harmonizing data privacy laws across EU member states and addressing challenges related to cross-border data transfers

Specialized Committees

SPC1 - UNHRC

UNITED NATIONS HUMAN RIGHTS COUNCIL

Agenda Item 1

Analyze the role of multinational corporations in preventing child labor within their supply chains and ensuring ethical practices

Agenda Item 2

Address the treatment of Uyghur Muslims in China, including reports of mass detentions, forced labor, and cultural suppression

Agenda Item 3

The Role of Digital Human Rights in Protecting Vulnerable Populations in Authoritarian Regimes

SPC2 - UNODC

THE UNITED NATIONS OFFICE ON DRUGS AND CRIME

Agenda Item 1

The Rise of "Ghost Guns" and 3D-Printed Firearms in Global Crime

Agenda Item 2

The Growing Threat of Drug-Resistant Synthetic Opioids

Agenda Item 3

The Use of Virtual Reality and the Metaverse for Criminal Activities

SPC3 - WHO

WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION

Agenda Item 1

Addressing the use of antibiotics in agriculture and its contribution to AMR, and promoting sustainable farming practices

Agenda Item 2

Implementing global initiatives to address the rising rates of mental health disorders, with a focus on integrating mental health services into primary healthcare

Agenda Item 3

Tackling the Global Obesity Epidemic: Public Health Policies and Prevention Strategies

SPC4 - UNWOMEN

UN WOMEN

Agenda Item 1

Combating femicide and honor killings through international legal frameworks

Agenda Item 2

Combating the Global Rise of Digital Misogyny and Incels

Agenda Item 3

The Feminization of Aging: Addressing the Socioeconomic Challenges Faced by Elderly Women

Other Committees

ECOSOC

ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL COMMITTEE

Agenda Item 1

Supporting female entrepreneurship, increasing women's participation in the workforce, and promoting gender equality in economic opportunities

Agenda Item 2

Ensuring food security through sustainable agricultural practices and rural development initiatives

Agenda Item 3

Ensuring access to quality education for refugee children in Jordan, focusing on integrating them into the national education system

UNSC

UNITED NATIONS SECURITY COUNCIL

Agenda Item 1

Mali-Burkina Faso Border Volatility: Addressing cross-border terrorism, communal violence, and territorial claims impacting the Sahel's stability

Agenda Item 2

Serbia-Kosovo Border Stability: Addressing ongoing disputes over recognition and border management, with a focus on EU mediation efforts
