About ARELMUN'25

Welcome to ARELMUN'25

AREL Model United Nations is an exceptional platform that brings together young minds from diverse backgrounds to engage in meaningful diplomatic dialogue. Our conference aims to foster leadership skills, critical thinking, and global awareness among participants.

With committees simulating various UN bodies and international organizations, delegates have the opportunity to debate pressing global issues, draft resolutions, and develop solutions to complex international problems.

Join us for an unforgettable experience that will challenge your perspectives, expand your horizons, and connect you with like-minded individuals from around the world.

Conference Highlights

Expert Committees

Simulate various UN bodies and international organizations with expert guidance.

Skill Development

Enhance public speaking, negotiation, and diplomacy skills in a realistic setting.

Global Network

Connect with delegates from diverse backgrounds and build lasting relationships.

Recognition

Outstanding delegates receive awards and certificates for their achievements.
