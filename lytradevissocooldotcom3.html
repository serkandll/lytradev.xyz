About USMS

Learn about our mission, values, and role within the ER:LC community

Justice, Integrity, Service

The United States Marshals Service is the nation's oldest and most versatile federal law enforcement agency. Since 1789, federal marshals have served the nation through dedication to the principles of justice, integrity, and service.

In Emergency Response: Liberty County, our USMS division focuses on fugitive apprehension, witness protection, prisoner transport, and specialized tactical operations. We work alongside other agencies to maintain law and order in Liberty County.

Our members are held to the highest standards of professionalism and are expected to exemplify the core values of the Marshals Service in all their interactions.

Our Operations

Specialized divisions and responsibilities within our ER:LC department

⚖️

Fugitive Apprehension

Tracking and capturing the most dangerous fugitives in Liberty County with precision and professionalism.

🛡️

Witness Protection

Ensuring the safety of key witnesses in high-profile cases through secure relocation and protection details.

🚔

Prisoner Transport

Secure transportation of federal prisoners between facilities with maximum security protocols.

🔫

Tactical Operations

Specialized response teams for high-risk warrants, hostage situations, and other critical incidents.

🔍

Asset Forfeiture

Managing seized assets from criminal enterprises and ensuring proper legal procedures are followed.

🏛️

Court Security

Protecting federal judiciary, courthouses, and ensuring the integrity of judicial proceedings.
