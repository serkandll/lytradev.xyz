About Our Division

Dedicated to Serving Liberty County

The Liberty County USMS Emergency Response Division was established to provide specialized support during critical incidents that threaten public safety and security.

Our highly trained personnel utilize state-of-the-art equipment and follow proven protocols to effectively respond to emergencies while minimizing risk to the community.

We work in close coordination with local law enforcement, fire departments, and emergency medical services to ensure a unified response to any crisis situation.
