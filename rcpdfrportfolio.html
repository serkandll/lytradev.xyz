About Lytradev

The name Lytradev actually started back in 2021 with just four friends and me. We were trying to build something while working on these servers, but being teenagers without much money or a reputation made it hard. We couldn't get the trust or the advertising we needed, so we ended up forgetting about that project.


In 2025, I got the team back together and we made it official. By then, we had the experience and resources to actually invest in proper advertising, a website, and team members. A big break came when one of our team members inherited his father's VDS firm, which now operates under our company. It really made things much easier for us as we did not have to pay for VDS's every month, and we could sell the rest.


So, Lytradev is not a new idea it's a reboot. We evolved, and that evolved Lytradev to what it is now.
